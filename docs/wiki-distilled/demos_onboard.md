# New User Onboarding Resources

Source: https://sciwiki.fredhutch.org/compdemos/onboard/

## Online Communities

Fred Hutch Slack workspace: Only @fredhutch.org email required (no invitation). Extends to Consortium members at UW and Children's Hospital.

## Support Organizations

- **Scientific Computing (SciComp)**: Research computing infrastructure
- **Office of the Chief Data Officer (OCDO)**: Data governance
- **Data Science Lab (DaSL)**: Data science support
- **IT Service Desk**: General IT support

## Events and Meetings

- **Data House Calls**: Schedule sessions with Data Science team for personalized guidance
- **Effective Computing Drop-In**: Biweekly Teams meeting with DaSL, SciComp, Data Core staff
- **SciComp Office Hours**: Weekly Teams meeting for research computing support

## Training

"Intro to Fred Hutch Cluster Computing" training course for those new to cluster computing. Wiki pathways section provides step-by-step task sequences.

## Documentation

SciWiki (sciwiki.fredhutch.org) hosts community-maintained documentation on computational tools, data generation, and analysis workflows.
